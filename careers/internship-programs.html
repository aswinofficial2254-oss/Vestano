About Vestano Internship Programs

Theory meets the floor. The Vestano internship is not about sitting at a desk; it is about active business operations.

3-month intensive

Department rotation

Interns move through Store Management, Inventory Control, business reporting, and operational support to understand how live retail teams work.

Mentorship

Department lead guidance

Every intern is paired with a Department Lead to understand how MIS is used for real-time business tracking and daily decision-making.

Goal

Operational readiness

The program turns fresh graduates into operationally ready professionals for retail, corporate, and consulting environments.
